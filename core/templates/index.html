Bio

Kinesic is an alternative rock band coming from Málaga (Spain). Since 2012, Kinesic has always been specialised on achieving the exact balance between a powerful sound and hypnotising ambience sections. The spirit of the band is to bring fresh and original ideas to the progressive rock scene, with songs crafted by musicians that share one concept: a whole life dedicated to do exactly this.

For the Nth Time

"They say repetition is the mother of learning. It’s like rubbing two sticks together to make a fire, it may take some time to master. And once the fire is made, it’s necessary to prevent sparks from flying out and making an unintentional second fire. But they also say that doing things over and over again won’t lead to different results. Maybe we just want to watch something burn uncontrollably. Maybe repetition will end up extinguishing the last creative spark. Or maybe they don’t know shit."
